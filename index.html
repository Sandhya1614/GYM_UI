Foundation
Hatha Yoga
All levels · 60 min

Hatha Yoga

The mother of all yoga styles. Hatha focuses on physical postures (asanas) and breathing techniques (pranayama) to align and calm the body, mind, and spirit.

  • Improves flexibility & posture
  • Reduces stress and anxiety
  • Builds core strength gradually
  • Suitable for complete beginners
Dynamic Flow
Vinyasa Flow
Intermediate · 60 min

Vinyasa Flow

A flowing, dynamic practice that links breath to movement. Each class is creatively sequenced for a different experience — no two sessions are the same.

  • Builds heat and cardiovascular fitness
  • Develops body awareness & rhythm
  • Improves muscular endurance
  • Best suited to intermediate practitioners
Heat & Power
Ashtanga Yoga
Advanced · 90 min

Ashtanga Yoga

A rigorous, structured practice with a fixed sequence of postures performed in the same order. Builds serious strength, stamina, and internal heat.

  • Rapid strength & endurance gains
  • Purifies through internal heat (tapas)
  • Traditional Mysore-style self-practice
  • Advanced practitioners preferred
Restoration
Yin Yoga
All levels · 75 min

Yin Yoga

Long-held passive poses that target the deep connective tissues. The perfect counterbalance to any active practice — deeply meditative and restorative.

  • Improves joint mobility & flexibility
  • Stimulates meridian channels
  • Deep relaxation response
  • Ideal after strength or HIIT sessions
Heated Practice
Bikram / Hot Yoga
All levels · 90 min

Hot Yoga

Practiced in a heated room (38–40°C), 26 postures and 2 breathing exercises. Intense detoxification with profound flexibility benefits.

  • Deep muscle relaxation in heat
  • Detoxification through sweat
  • Accelerated calorie burn
  • Cardio conditioning benefits
Alignment-Based
Iyengar Yoga
Beginner-Intermediate · 75 min

Iyengar Yoga

Precision-led practice with extensive use of props — belts, blocks, bolsters — to achieve perfect alignment. Ideal for those recovering from injury.

  • Perfect anatomical alignment
  • Therapeutic for chronic pain
  • Props make it highly accessible
  • Develops attention and discipline
Mind & Body
Kundalini Yoga
All levels · 75 min

Kundalini Yoga

Known as the yoga of awareness. Combines breathwork, mantra, mudra, and movement to awaken dormant energy and expand consciousness.

  • Energises nervous system
  • Powerful breathwork (pranayama)
  • Mantra and meditation included
  • Transformative inner experience
Women's Wellness
Prenatal Yoga
Expecting moms · 60 min

Prenatal Yoga

A safe, nurturing practice designed for all trimesters. Focuses on breathing for labour, pelvic floor strength, and the emotional journey of pregnancy.

  • Safe for all trimesters
  • Reduces pregnancy discomfort
  • Breathing for labour preparation
  • Supportive community of mothers
Aerial Practice
Aerial Yoga
Beginner-Advanced · 60 min

Aerial Yoga

Traditional yoga poses adapted using a silk hammock suspended from the ceiling. Decompresses the spine and allows inversions with full support.

  • Full spinal decompression
  • Supported inversions for everyone
  • Core and upper body strength
  • Incredibly fun and playful

Our Story

Who We Are

Founded in 2016, Zenith Studio began as a 2,000 sq.ft. yoga room with three instructors and a vision: to create a wellness space in Chennai where elite training and mindful practice existed side by side, under one elegant roof.

Today, we operate across 12,000 sq.ft. with 24 certified trainers, 15 yoga varieties, a dedicated nutrition clinic, and a recovery lounge — having transformed the lives of over 1,800 active members.

Our philosophy is simple: real transformation happens when the body, mind, and spirit work together. Everything at Zenith is designed with that belief at its core.
